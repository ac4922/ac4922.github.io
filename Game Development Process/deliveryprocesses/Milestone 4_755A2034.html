Milestone: Milestone 4
The assignment is to finish your game for this milestone. In case of extra time, polish the game, add extra levels or implement any of your “extra” features. In addition to wrapping up the project and making sure all documentation is accurate, work as a team to complete a project post-mortem. By the end of milestone 4 have a polished game that includes all of your MVP (minimum viable product) features. Document any known issues!
